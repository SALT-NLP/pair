Instructions     (Expand/Collapse)

This is a study on the use of search engines to understand difficult opinion questions. We anticipate that the scientific findings of this study will help advance the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Information Retrieval, and Psychology.

You will be asked to read a subjective query in the QUERY box below. Then you will answer Question (1) to tell us about your opinion on the topic. On a scale from -3 to 3, do you agree or disagree with the PERSPECTIVE to the QUERY. For example, with the following...

QUERY: Does SeaWorld provide adequate care for its marine animals?

PERSPECTIVE: Perspective 1: SeaWorld is providing adequate care for its marine animals

If you slightly believe SeaWorld DOES provide adequate care, you might mark +1 (moderately agree); but if you strongly believe SeaWorld fails to provide the right care, you should mark -3 (strongly disagree). If you are neutral or don't know, just mark 0.

Next, we will ask you to summarize your own answer to the QUERY in Question (2), based on what you know and believe. Before you give us this summary, however, you are required to use our built-in search engine to do more background research. Just click the "Search" button and click the links to various articles, just like you would with a regular Google Search. Don't use any external tools to do any additional research.

When you write your opinion summary, you will be expected to provide one or two complete / grammatical sentences of meaningful commentary. We have automatic filters that will block HIT submissions if your summary is too short, sparse, disorganized, ungrammatical, or uncritical. We will also block your account if we have reason to believe you are using Generative AI tools like ChatGPT to answer this field.

Finally, you will complete a final survey question.

Participation in this research is voluntary, and you are free to withdraw your consent at any time.

TIME INVOLVEMENT: Your participation will take approximately 5-10 minutes per task to complete.
